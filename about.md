# About Everyday Defender

It all started in 2019 - Chris and Koos met at a conference in Haarlem, the Netherlands. Since then, our friendship has grown as has our passion for sharing our experiences with the community.

Our goal for the Everyday Defender podcast is to provide snackable, practical tips and tricks based on our real-world experience to help you secure your Microsoft cloud environments.
