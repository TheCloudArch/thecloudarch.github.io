Nic and Chris catch up with Philip Griffiths about next-generation WAN being delivered over the internet, how perimeter security is not everything and the challenge of working in a global networking company.


<img src="/assets/images/uploads/2019/08/phill.jpeg" alt="Philip Griffiths" class="img-left"> Philip Griffiths is Head of EMEA Partnerships and Alliances for NetFoundry and regularly speaks at events from DevOps to IoT to Cyber Security. Prior to this, he worked for Atos IT Services in various roles working with C-suit executives to realise their digital transformation. He lives in London with his wife and daughter and enjoys travelling for both work or pleasure.
